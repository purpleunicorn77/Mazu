Chapter 1 Qianliyan and Shunfeng'er

According to legend, Mazu subdued two powerful beings from Peach Mountain who terrorized the villagers: Qianliyan (千里眼), a green-faced monster with the ability to see a thousand miles, and Shunfeng'er (順風耳), a red-faced monster with extraordinary hearing. After their defeat, both became her devoted servants.
